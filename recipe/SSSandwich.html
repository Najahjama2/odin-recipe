Smoked Salmon Sandwich Recipe

Home

Description

The Smoked Salmon Sandwich is so underrated its truly a gem it's nutrious and very quick and easy to make!

Ingredients:

  • Sourdough bread
  • Smoked Salmon
  • Cream cheese
  • Any veggies of your choice really I recommend cucumbers, red onions and or lettuce
  • Capers

    • The following are optional:
  • Dill , chives or any other herbs
  • Lemon

    • Steps:

    1. This is totally optional but I liked to start with my sourdough slices toasted
    2. Next we spread our cream cheese on our toasted slices
    3. After this step we add our smoked salmon
    4. Lastly we add our veggies of choice and the capers perfect time to add our optional ingredients too!
    5. And voila! truly the easiest most yummiest sandwich to make
